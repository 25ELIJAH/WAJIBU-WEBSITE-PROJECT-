About WAJIBU

We Built This Because We have Sat On Both Sides Of The Review.

WAJIBU was founded by regulatory lawyers and GRC professionals who have built compliance frameworks from inside regulated organisations. We know what the FCA looks for. We know what a Section 166 review feels like from inside the firm. And we know exactly what falls apart under scrutiny, because we have seen it.

We do not sell templates. We build defences. Regulatory fines are the minimum. Reputational damage, investor withdrawal, and loss of FCA authorisation are the ceiling. Our mandate is to make sure you never find out how high that ceiling is.

How We Work

Our Compliance Methodology

A structured approach that takes you from obligation to evidence, with nothing left to chance.

01
Obligation Mapping

We identify every regulatory obligation that applies to your business, sector and operating model before we do anything else.

02
Exposure Review

We assess where your current policies, processes and documentation fall short of what is required. No gaps are left unaddressed.

03
Framework Design

We architect the governance structures, policies and controls your business needs to satisfy its obligations under current regulations.

04
Policy Architecture

We write the policies and procedures that translate regulatory requirements into practical, enforceable internal standards.

05
Evidence Generation

We produce the documentation, reports and audit trails that demonstrate compliance in a form that regulators and investors will accept.

06
Regulatory Readiness

We keep your compliance programme current as the FCA, CSRD, and wider regulatory landscape continues to evolve.
