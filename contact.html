FAQs

Common Questions

How long does the assessment call take?

Assessment calls are 30 minutes. Samuel will use the time to understand your current compliance obligations, identify your exposure, and tell you plainly what needs to happen next. No obligation to proceed.

Is the assessment call really free?

Completely free, with no strings attached. We offer it because we believe the right relationship starts with understanding your actual compliance position, not a sales pitch.

How quickly will I receive a response to my enquiry?

Samuel reads every enquiry personally and responds within one business day. For urgent matters, call directly on +44 7369 252639.

What happens after I book a meeting?

You and Samuel both receive an email confirmation with the meeting details. For video calls, a Zoom link is included. Samuel may send a short pre-call questionnaire to make the session as productive as possible.

How is my data protected?

All form data is transmitted over HTTPS and handled in full compliance with UK GDPR. We use Web3Forms for secure form processing. Your data is never sold or shared with third parties.
